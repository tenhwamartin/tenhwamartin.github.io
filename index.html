About Us

ESACCO is an association that makes it easy for individuals espescially in our local communities to access loans cheaply ,save their money conviently and invest in their future desires. This idea was discovered by a group of Makerere students who found the urge to solve the lack of accountability challenge in the community.

Team Work Makes The Dream Work

As ESACCO we believe if we come together as one community and support each other we can achieve great things through this program.
